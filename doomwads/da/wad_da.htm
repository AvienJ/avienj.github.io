Death Arena

My attempt at making a survival style map, monsters infinitly spawn until the time limit hits. If you run out of ammo, head to the resupply portal and re-stock, don't stay in there too long! Once you are in there the timer pauses and monsters can walk through the portal as well. There are two alternate builds of this map, one with no timer, and one that has set the skill up to the MAX! Only for true gamers only!


Enjoy!
